Lyricon Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-07-15

Lyricon shows synced lyrics for the song you are currently playing. This policy explains what the app does and does not do with your data. Lyricon has no user accounts and no analytics or tracking.

Data we collect

None. Lyricon does not collect, store, or transmit personal data to us. We operate no servers that receive your data, and we do not track you across apps or websites.

Data that stays on your device

  • Settings (theme, sync offset, language choices) are saved locally on your device.
  • Cached lyrics, romanizations, and translations are stored locally so previously seen songs load faster.
  • Your Spotify sign-in token (only if you choose to connect Spotify) is stored securely in the iOS Keychain on your device and is never sent to us.

Third-party services

  • LRCLIB (lrclib.net): to find lyrics, the app sends the current song's title, artist, and duration to LRCLIB. This request is not linked to your identity.
  • Spotify (optional): if you connect Spotify, the app reads your current playback position and track info to sync lyrics. It does not stream audio or post to your account. Your use of Spotify is also governed by Spotify's Privacy Policy.
  • Apple Music / Now Playing: the app reads the current playback position from the system to sync lyrics. This stays on your device.
  • Apple Translation framework: translations run on-device using Apple's Translation framework; downloading a language pack is handled by iOS.

Children

Lyricon is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone.
